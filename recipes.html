Chicken Fajita Pizza

Ingredients

Pizza dough, Grilled chicken, Bell peppers, Onions, Mozzarella cheese, Pizza sauce.

Steps to Prepare

Spread sauce on dough. Add toppings and cheese. Bake at 200°C for 15 mins until golden.

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

Fresh Basil, Tomato sauce, Fresh Mozzarella, Olive oil, Sea salt.

Steps to Prepare

Layer sauce and mozzarella. Bake until crust is brown. Garnish with fresh basil and olive oil.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

Basmati rice, Chicken, Yogurt, Biryani masala, Fried onions, Mint leaves.

Steps to Prepare

Cook chicken masala. Boil rice with spices. Layer rice and chicken, then steam (Dum) for 15 mins.

Beef Nihari

Ingredients

Beef shank, Wheat flour, Nihari spices, Ginger, Lemon, Green chilies.

Steps to Prepare

Slow cook beef with spices for 6 hours. Add flour mix to thicken. Garnish with ginger and lemon.

Hot & Sour Soup

Ingredients

Chicken broth, Vinegar, Soy sauce, Mushrooms, Tofu, Egg drops, Black pepper.

Steps to Prepare

Boil broth with veggies. Add sauces and cornflour. Whisk in egg slowly and serve hot.

Chicken Chow Mein

Ingredients

Noodles, Cabbage, Carrots, Soy sauce, Chicken strips, Garlic, Oyster sauce.

Steps to Prepare

Boil noodles. Stir fry chicken and veggies on high heat. Mix everything with sauces.

Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients

Dark chocolate, Butter, Eggs, Sugar, Flour, Vanilla essence.

Steps to Prepare

Melt chocolate and butter. Whisk eggs and sugar. Fold in flour and bake for 12 mins at 180°C.
